WeRadical Rafting

WeRadical is your ultimate destination for an adrenaline-packed whitewater rafting experience in the breathtaking landscapes of Alberta, Canada. As a rapidly growing company, we pride ourselves on safety, expertise, and a passion for outdoor adventure.

History

In 2012, WeRadical was born in the rugged wilderness of Alberta, Canada, founded by outdoor enthusiasts determined to redefine adventure. Beginning with a handful of passionate guides, the company rapidly gained recognition for its commitment to safety and technology. As of 2024, WeRadical stands as a beacon for those craving adrenaline-packed adventures, reflecting the founders' vision to make every journey with WeRadical a transformative and unforgettable experience. The history of WeRadical is a testament to a relentless pursuit of adventure, safety, and the unyielding spirit of exploration.
