WeRadical Trips

Wilderness Explorer Expedition

Embark on a multi-day journey deep into the heart of Alberta's untamed wilderness. Traverse roaring rapids, camp under starlit skies, and discover hidden gems only accessible by raft. Led by expert guides, this immersive adventure promises unforgettable moments and lifelong memories.

Extreme Rapids Challenge

For the adrenaline junkies seeking the ultimate thrill, our Extreme Rapids Challenge is a must-do. Conquer some of Alberta's most formidable rapids, navigating through swirling waters and exhilarating drops. With top-of-the-line equipment and expert guidance, this high-intensity adventure guarantees an adrenaline-fueled experience like no other.

Family Fun Day on the River

Looking for a memorable outing the whole family can enjoy? Our Family Fun Day on the River is the perfect choice. Suitable for adventurers of all ages, this guided rafting trip offers gentle rapids, breathtaking scenery, and plenty of opportunities for laughter and bonding. With safety as our top priority, you can relax and create cherished moments with your loved ones amidst the beauty of nature.
